Team History

The Dallas Mavericks are a professional basketball team based in Dallas, Texas. Founded in 1980, they have won one NBA Championship (2011) and have been home to numerous NBA legends.

Notable Players

  • Luka Dončić
  • Dirk Nowitzki
  • Jason Kidd
  • Steve Nash

Home Stadium

The Mavericks play their home games at the American Airlines Center in Dallas. It has a seating capacity of over 20,000 for basketball games and hosts concerts and other events.
